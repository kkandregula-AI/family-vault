Summary: Family Vault stores all your documents privately on your own device and/or your own Google Drive account. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data or documents.

1. What Family Vault Is

Family Vault is a free, open-source Progressive Web App (PWA) that helps individuals and families securely store and organise important documents such as passports, medical records, financial documents, and legal papers.

The app runs entirely in your browser. There is no server, no backend, and no central database.

2. Data Storage Your device only

All data you enter into Family Vault is stored in one or more of the following locations ‚Äî all of which are under your direct control:

  • Browser storage (localStorage / IndexedDB) ‚Äî stored locally on your device, inside your browser
  • Your Google Drive account ‚Äî if you choose to connect Google Drive, files are saved directly to your personal Drive. Only you have access.
  • A folder on your device ‚Äî if you link a local folder (Android/PC), files are saved there directly

The app developer has no access to any of this data at any time.

3. Google Drive Integration

Family Vault can optionally connect to Google Drive to back up your documents. When you connect Google Drive:

  • You authorise the app using your own Google account via Google's secure OAuth 2.0 sign-in
  • Files are saved to a "Family Vault" folder inside your own Google Drive
  • The app only requests permission to manage files it creates ‚Äî it cannot access any other files in your Drive
  • Your Google credentials are never stored by the app ‚Äî only a temporary access token held in your browser session
  • You can disconnect Google Drive at any time from Settings

Google Drive usage is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

Family Vault does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Document contents or metadata
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Cookies (no tracking cookies of any kind)

5. PIN & Security

The PIN you set to lock the app is stored only on your device as a one-way encoded value. It is never transmitted anywhere. The secret word used for PIN recovery is similarly stored only on your device.

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts ‚Äî for typography (Nunito, Libre Baskerville). Font files are loaded from Google's CDN.
  • Google OAuth 2.0 ‚Äî only when you choose to connect Google Drive
  • Google Drive API ‚Äî only when you choose to connect Google Drive

No other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks are used.

7. Children's Privacy

Family Vault does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is a document storage tool intended for adults managing family records.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  • Browser data: Clear your browser's site data for this app, or uninstall the PWA
  • Google Drive data: Delete the "Family Vault" folder from your Google Drive
  • Device folder: Delete the linked folder from your phone's file manager
